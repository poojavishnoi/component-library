Navigation

Navigation bar is used to navigate through the web apps or websites. This navigation component is responsive. It is a hamburger menu in medium and small devices

Example of navigation

On L and XL screens
  • Brand Logo
  • Navigation links
  • Search bar
  • Section to show icons and text below
On M,S and XS screens
  • Brand title
  • Hamburger section
  • Items of navigation links will be included inside hamburger section
  • Section to show icons without text
